Welcome

My name is Shadi Ghandehari

A designer who specializes in fashion, graphics, Adobe Suite, VS Code and UX/UI design.

S

Shadi is a designer based in Toronto, Ontario, and is deeply passionate about design and specializes in various areas, including illustration, fashion design, graphic design, UX/UI design, and most recently, VS Code.

As a perpetual learner, Shadi is always on the lookout for new ways to grow and enhance her skills. She loves exploring different design industries as it allows her to gain fresh insights and inspiration. By diversifying her skill set, she can create unique and effective designs that cater to her client's needs.

Shadi is committed to infusing a personal touch into every project she works on and creating a lasting impression with her designs.
